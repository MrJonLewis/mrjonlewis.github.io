Collaboration tools

How we work together is what creates value in our business. This is why Zengenti is a company of people, not a collection. All of our values influence the way we collaborate:

  1. We believe in each other
  2. Be open
  3. Put energy into how we communicate
  4. Don’t screw the customer
  5. Don’t be afraid to make mistakes
  6. We take pride in our creations

Use the tools here to refine and evolve the way you work here. Occasionally we rely too much on the traditional meeting format, dig around here to find alternatives that are often more meaningful and effective uses of our time and mental focus.
